The Divorce Act,1869

The act does not apply in State of Goa and Union Territories of Daman and Diu. No decree for dissolution of a marriage can be granted under the Indian Divorce Act, 1869 unless the parties are domiciled in India. A decree for nullity of marriage can be granted if the marriage was solemnized in India and petitioner was resident in India, when petition was presented.
